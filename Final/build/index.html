TRIGGERS

  • Hover on "Download"
  • Click on "Download"

RULES

  • Hover on "Download"
    • Music notes fly out from
      behind the button
    • Notes fade out after some time
    • Cursor changes to pointer
  • Click on "Download"
    • "Download" button changes to
      a lighter blue while active
    • Download modal expands and fades
      in from the "Download" button
    • Black background appears but
      stays transparent
    • Cursor changes to pointer over
      menu items, back to normal after
      hovering off
    • Clicking the "X" or off the modal
      causes the modal to shrink back
      into the download button and the
      background to disappear
  • Hover on options in modal
    • Light-blue background appears
  • Click on options in modal
    • Modal closes
    • A circle with a line and a dot
      appear, dot moves to the top
    • The stroke of the circle turns
      white after a bit
    • The line turns into a checkmark
    • "Downloaded file" will be logged in
      the console
    • Checkmark stays till modal is
      opened again or page is reloaded
